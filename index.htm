Master Your Outlook

Simple trick to regain control.

Outlook Filter Speedrun

45 seconds • Watch now

1

Right Click is Key

Find an annoying email. Right click it. Select Rules > Create Rule.

2

Move to Folder

Tell Outlook to move all emails from this sender to a specific folder (e.g., "Newsletters").

3

Color Code the Boss

Go to View Settings > Conditional Formatting. Make emails from specific people turn RED.
